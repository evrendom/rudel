# Spotify Source Notes

These are the primary sources behind this skill. Use them for rationale, not for cargo-culting aesthetics.

- `2025 user experience`
  More layers, controls, hub/feed, clips, clubs, archive, and party.
  https://newsroom.spotify.com/2025-12-03/2025-wrapped-user-experience/

- `2025 methodology explainer`
  Per-story metric choice, filters, cutoffs, and why results can surprise users.
  https://newsroom.spotify.com/2025-12-05/wrapped-methodology-explained/

- `2025 how it is made`
  Eligible users, filtered data, archive logic, and the “accurate, fair, reflective, but still magical” framing.

  Narrative-potential ranking, heuristics, LLM creative contract, and evaluation at scale.

- `Listening Personality engineering post`

- `Interactive experiences year-round`
  Social-first thinking, experimentation, fan participation, and the role of playful personalized moments beyond the annual recap.

- `2024 Music Evolution`
  Distinct phases, human-plus-ML curation, significance over completeness, and beat-specific eligibility.
  https://newsroom.spotify.com/2024-12-04/everything-you-need-to-know-about-your-music-evolution/

- `2022 Wrapped`
  Personality, Audio Day, artist messages, richer sharing surfaces, and non-app extensions.
  https://newsroom.spotify.com/2022-11-30/everything-you-need-to-know-about-2022-wrapped/

- `2019 in-app engineering post`
  Clear goals, easy sharing, launch discipline, localization issues, and odd-data edge cases.
  https://engineering.atspotify.com/2020/9/spotify-unwrapped-2019-how-we-built-an-in-app-experience-just-for-you

- `2025 Wrapped for artists, creators, authors, advertisers`
  Different protagonists need different recap systems, not just re-skinned versions of one template.
  https://newsroom.spotify.com/2025-12-03/wrapped-for-artists-songwriters-creators-authors-advertisers/
